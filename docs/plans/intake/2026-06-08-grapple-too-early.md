## What we agreed on

The tutorial and level progression are currently pointing at grapple too early. Grapple is an Armchair ability and should not be presented as the next required action while the player is still Basic Chair. Before the boss/unlock moment, the player should be guided toward the reachable Basic Chair path: practice basics, talk to the NPC if needed, then reach/fight ReclinerBaron. Only after Armchair is unlocked should the level point the player toward grapple markers, the Armchair gate, and Platform 3.

This is a progression clarity/blocker fix. It is not a request to change grapple physics, boss combat, form unlock rules, or add a new ability. The fix should make it obvious that grapple is later, and make sure the player can make forward progress before having it.

## Acceptance criteria

- The opening tutorial does not ask the player to use Special/Grapple before Armchair is unlocked.
- Any visible grapple labels/signposts in the opening view are hidden, moved later, or worded clearly as locked/later content until after the player earns Armchair.
- The player can see a reachable Basic Chair path from the safe/training area toward the NPC and then toward ReclinerBaron without needing grapple.
- The level does not visually imply that the first required path is blocked by an Armchair gate or grapple-only route.
- After Armchair unlock, the game may then point the player toward Special/Grapple and the yellow markers.
- Existing mechanics remain intact: Basic movement, attack, NPC, boss unlock, Armchair form switching, and grapple behavior.
